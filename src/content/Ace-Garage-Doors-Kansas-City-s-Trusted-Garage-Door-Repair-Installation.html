Expert Garage Door Repair Services

Garage doors are complex systems that can encounter a variety of issues. Our comprehensive repair services cover everything from minor adjustments to major overhauls. We specialize in diagnosing and fixing common problems to ensure your garage door operates smoothly and safely.

Torsion Spring Replacement and Extension Spring Repair

Broken garage door springs are one of the most common and potentially dangerous issues. The springs are under immense tension and are crucial for the balanced operation of your garage door. We offer expert torsion spring replacement and extension spring repair to restore your door's functionality and safety. Ignoring a broken spring can lead to further damage and pose a safety risk, so it's essential to address it promptly. Our technicians are trained to handle all types of spring repairs safely and efficiently.

Garage Door Cable Repair and Roller Replacement

Worn or snapped garage door cables can prevent your door from opening or closing properly. We provide professional garage door cable repair services to ensure your door operates smoothly. Similarly, worn-out rollers can cause your garage door to become noisy, stick, or even derail. Our garage door roller replacement service will ensure your door glides effortlessly along its tracks.

Garage Door Panel Replacement and Track Alignment

Accidents happen, and sometimes garage door panels can become damaged. Whether it's from a vehicle impact or severe weather, we offer reliable garage door panel replacement to restore the appearance and integrity of your door. Misaligned tracks can cause your garage door to bind or become difficult to operate. Our technicians are skilled in precise garage door track alignment to ensure smooth and safe movement.

Garage Door Weatherstripping and Opener Services

Protect your garage and its contents from the elements with our high-quality garage door weatherstripping services. Properly sealed weatherstripping prevents drafts, moisture, and pests from entering your garage. We also service and repair a wide range of garage door openers, including popular brands like LiftMaster opener and Chamberlain opener. Whether your opener is slow, noisy, or completely unresponsive, we can diagnose the problem and provide the necessary repairs or replacement.

Expert Garage Door Repair Services

Garage doors are complex systems with many moving parts, and when something goes wrong, it can be a major disruption. Our team is equipped to handle a wide range of issues, ensuring your garage door operates smoothly and safely. We specialize in:

Torsion Spring Replacement & Extension Spring Repair

Broken garage door springs are one of the most common and dangerous issues homeowners face. Springs are under immense tension, and a snapped spring can cause significant damage and pose a serious safety risk. Ace Garage Doors offers professional torsion spring replacement and extension spring repair services. Our technicians will quickly and safely replace your broken springs, restoring your garage door's balance and functionality. We also provide guidance on preventative maintenance to extend the life of your springs.

Garage Door Opener Repair & Installation (LiftMaster & Chamberlain)

Is your garage door opener acting up? Whether you have a LiftMaster opener or a Chamberlain opener, our experts can diagnose and fix a variety of problems. From remote control issues and strange noises to complete failure, we provide expert repair and installation services. Garage Door Cable Repair & Roller Replacement

Frayed or broken garage door cables can lead to an unbalanced door and potential safety hazards. Similarly, worn-out rollers can cause your door to move unevenly or get stuck. Ace Garage Doors provides prompt and reliable garage door cable repair and garage door roller replacement. Garage Door Panel Replacement & Track Alignment

Damaged garage door panels can detract from your home's curb appeal and compromise its security. Whether a panel is dented, cracked, or rotted, we offer efficient garage door panel replacement services to restore its appearance and integrity. Garage Door Weatherstripping Installation

Proper garage door weatherstripping is crucial for protecting your home from the elements, preventing drafts, and improving energy efficiency. Worn or damaged weatherstripping can allow water, pests, and temperature fluctuations to enter your garage and home. We offer professional installation of high-quality weatherstripping to create a tight seal, keeping your garage comfortable and secure year-round.

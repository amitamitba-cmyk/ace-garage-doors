Comprehensive Garage Door Repair Services

At Ace Garage Doors, we offer a full spectrum of repair services to address any issue you might encounter with your garage door system. We pride ourselves on our ability to diagnose problems quickly and provide lasting solutions. Our services include:

Spring Replacements: Torsion & Extension Springs

Garage door springs are crucial for the balanced operation of your door. When they break, it can render your garage door inoperable and potentially dangerous. We specialize in expert torsion spring replacement and extension spring repair. Our technicians carry a wide variety of springs to ensure we can fix your door promptly. Don't let a broken spring disrupt your day; we offer fast and reliable spring repair services.

Opener Repair & Installation: LiftMaster & Chamberlain Specialists

Is your garage door opener acting up? Whether you have a LiftMaster opener or a Chamberlain opener, our skilled technicians are equipped to handle repairs and installations for all major brands. We can diagnose issues ranging from remote control problems to motor malfunctions. If it's time for an upgrade, we can also recommend and install the latest, most reliable garage door openers to enhance your convenience and security.

Cable Repair & Roller Replacement

Worn or broken garage door cables can cause the door to sag or become difficult to lift. We provide professional garage door cable repair to restore proper function and safety. Similarly, damaged or worn-out rollers can lead to noisy operation and strain on other components. Our garage door roller replacement service ensures your door glides smoothly along its tracks.

Panel Replacement & Track Alignment

Accidents happen, and sometimes garage door panels can become damaged. We offer efficient garage door panel replacement to restore the aesthetic appeal and structural integrity of your door. If your door is off-track or experiencing uneven movement, our technicians can perform precise garage door track alignment to ensure smooth and safe operation.

Weatherstripping & Sealing

Protect your garage and its contents from the elements with our professional garage door weatherstripping services. Properly sealed doors prevent drafts, moisture, and pests from entering your garage, helping to maintain a comfortable temperature and protect your belongings.

Comprehensive Garage Door Repair Services

At Ace Garage Doors, we offer a full spectrum of repair services designed to get your garage door back in optimal working condition. Our expertise covers:

Spring Replacements: Torsion and Extension Springs

Garage door springs are crucial for the safe operation of your door. Over time, they can weaken and break. We specialize in expert torsion spring replacement and extension spring repair. A broken spring can prevent your door from opening or closing, and attempting to replace it yourself can be dangerous. Trust our professionals to handle this critical repair safely and effectively. We can also advise on the best type of spring for your specific garage door system.

Opener Repair and Installation: LiftMaster & Chamberlain Specialists

Is your garage door opener acting up? Whether you have a LiftMaster opener or a Chamberlain opener, our technicians are trained to diagnose and repair a wide range of issues. We handle everything from remote control problems and sensor malfunctions to motor issues and complete opener replacement. We also offer professional installation of new openers, ensuring smooth and quiet operation.

Cable and Roller Replacements

Worn or broken garage door cables can cause the door to sag or become unbalanced. Similarly, damaged rollers can lead to noisy operation and difficulty moving the door. We provide reliable garage door cable repair and efficient garage door roller replacement to ensure your door moves smoothly and safely along its tracks.

Panel Replacement and Track Alignment

Accidents happen, and sometimes garage door panels can become damaged. We offer seamless garage door panel replacement to restore the appearance and integrity of your door. If your door is off track or struggling to move, our technicians can perform precise garage door track alignment to correct the issue and prevent further damage.

Weatherstripping and Sealing

Protect your home from the elements with proper garage door weatherstripping. Worn-out seals can allow drafts, moisture, and pests to enter your garage. We can replace old or damaged weatherstripping to ensure a tight seal, improving your home's energy efficiency and security.

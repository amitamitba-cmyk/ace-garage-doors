Common Garage Door Issues We Address:

Torsion Spring Replacement & Extension Spring Repair

Garage door springs are essential for the balanced operation of your door. When they break, the door becomes extremely heavy and difficult, if not impossible, to lift. We specialize in expert torsion spring replacement and extension spring repair. Recognizing the signs of a failing spring, such as a loud snap or a door that won't stay open, is crucial. Ignoring these can lead to further damage or safety hazards. We ensure your new springs are correctly tensioned for optimal performance and safety.

Garage Door Cable Repair & Roller Replacement

Worn or broken cables can cause your garage door to sag or become inoperable. Our technicians are adept at garage door cable repair, ensuring your door lifts and lowers evenly. Similarly, old or damaged rollers can lead to noisy operation and strain on your garage door system. We provide efficient garage door roller replacement to restore smooth, quiet movement.

Garage Door Panel Replacement & Track Alignment

Accidents happen, and sometimes garage door panels get damaged. Whether it's from a vehicle impact or severe weather, we offer professional garage door panel replacement to restore the aesthetic and structural integrity of your door. Misaligned tracks can cause the door to bind or come off its rollers. Our team performs precise garage door track alignment to ensure seamless operation and prevent further issues.

LiftMaster & Chamberlain Opener Service

As authorized dealers and experienced technicians, we handle a wide range of garage door openers, including popular brands like LiftMaster opener and Chamberlain opener. Whether you need a new opener installed, an existing one repaired, or just need help programming a remote, we have you covered. We can diagnose and fix issues related to remote control, safety sensors, and motor performance.

Garage Door Weatherstripping & Emergency Service

Protect your home from the elements with proper garage door weatherstripping. Worn-out seals can allow drafts, moisture, and pests into your garage. We replace old weatherstripping to improve energy efficiency and security. When unexpected problems arise, our emergency garage door service is available to address urgent issues like broken springs or doors that won't close, ensuring your property remains secure. We also offer same-day garage door repair for many common problems.

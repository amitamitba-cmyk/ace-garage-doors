Expert Garage Door Repair Services

A malfunctioning garage door can be a major inconvenience and a security risk. At Ace Garage Doors, we specialize in diagnosing and repairing a wide array of common and complex garage door problems. Our services include:

Torsion Spring Replacement & Extension Spring Repair

Garage door springs are crucial for the safe operation of your door. When they break, the door becomes extremely heavy and difficult to lift. We offer professional torsion spring replacement and extension spring repair to restore your garage door's balance and functionality. Ignoring a broken spring can lead to further damage and safety hazards, so it's essential to address this issue promptly. We ensure the correct spring type and tension are used for optimal performance and longevity.

Garage Door Cable Repair

Frayed or snapped garage door cables can prevent your door from opening or closing correctly. Our technicians are adept at garage door cable repair, ensuring your cables are properly tensioned and secured. We use high-quality replacement parts to guarantee a durable and safe repair.

Garage Door Panel Replacement

Damaged or dented garage door panels can detract from your home's curb appeal and compromise security. Whether due to impact, weather, or age, we provide efficient garage door panel replacement services. We can match existing panel styles or help you choose a new look for your garage door.

Garage Door Roller Replacement

Worn-out or broken rollers can cause your garage door to move unevenly, make loud noises, or even get stuck. Replacing old rollers with new ones is a cost-effective way to improve your garage door's performance. We offer expert garage door roller replacement to ensure smooth and quiet operation.

Garage Door Track Alignment

If your garage door is off-track or struggling to move smoothly, it might need garage door track alignment. Misaligned tracks can cause significant wear and tear on other components. Our technicians can quickly identify and correct track issues, ensuring your door operates safely within its guides.

Garage Door Weatherstripping

Protect your home from the elements with proper garage door weatherstripping. Worn-out seals can allow drafts, moisture, and pests to enter your garage. We install and replace weatherstripping to create a tight seal, improving energy efficiency and protecting your belongings.

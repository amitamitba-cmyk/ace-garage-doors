Spring Replacements: Torsion and Extension Springs

Garage door springs are vital components that bear the weight of the door, allowing it to open and close smoothly. Over time, these springs can weaken or break, posing a safety hazard and preventing the door from functioning. We offer expert torsion spring replacement and extension spring repair services. Our technicians are skilled in safely handling and replacing these high-tension parts. If you're wondering about broken spring repair cost, rest assured that we provide competitive pricing and transparent quotes. Don't let a broken spring leave you stranded; call us for prompt service.

Garage Door Opener Repair and Installation

Modern garage doors rely on efficient openers for convenience and security. Whether you have a LiftMaster opener or a Chamberlain opener, our technicians are equipped to handle repairs, maintenance, and new installations. We can troubleshoot issues like openers not responding, slow operation, or strange noises. If it's time for an upgrade, we can help you choose and install the latest opener models that offer enhanced features and reliability.

Comprehensive Cable and Roller Services

The cables and rollers on your garage door are essential for its smooth movement along the tracks. Worn-out rollers can cause jerky movements and excessive noise, while frayed or snapped cables can lead to the door becoming inoperable or falling unevenly. We provide professional garage door cable repair and garage door roller replacement to ensure your door operates flawlessly. Proper maintenance of these components can prevent more significant issues down the line.

Addressing Damaged Panels and Track Issues

Accidents happen, and sometimes garage door panels can become dented or damaged. We offer garage door panel replacement to restore the appearance and integrity of your door. Additionally, if your garage door is off-track or the tracks are misaligned, it can cause significant operational problems and safety risks. Our technicians are adept at garage door track alignment to ensure your door moves smoothly and securely.

Weatherstripping and Sealing

Protect your home from the elements with proper garage door weatherstripping. Worn or damaged weather seals around the perimeter of your garage door can allow drafts, moisture, and pests to enter your garage and home. We can replace old weatherstripping to create a tight seal, improving energy efficiency and protecting your belongings.

Comprehensive Garage Door Repair Services

A malfunctioning garage door can be more than just an annoyance; it can be a security risk and a major inconvenience. Ace Garage Doors offers a full spectrum of repair services to get your door back in optimal working condition:

Spring Replacements: Torsion and Extension Springs

Garage door springs are under immense tension and are crucial for the smooth operation of your door. When they break, the door becomes extremely heavy and difficult, if not impossible, to open. We specialize in expert torsion spring replacement and extension spring repair. Our technicians can quickly diagnose spring issues and perform safe, efficient replacements to restore your garage door's balance and functionality. Don't risk injury attempting a DIY spring repair; trust the professionals at Ace Garage Doors.

Opener Repair and Installation: LiftMaster & Chamberlain Specialists

Is your garage door opener struggling to lift the door, making strange noises, or not responding at all? We are experts in repairing and installing a wide range of garage door openers, including popular brands like LiftMaster opener and Chamberlain opener systems. Whether it's a faulty logic board, a worn-out gear, or a remote control issue, we can diagnose and fix the problem, or help you choose and install a new, reliable opener.

Cable Repair and Roller Replacement

Worn or broken garage door cables can cause the door to sag or become unstable. Similarly, old or damaged rollers can lead to noisy operation and strain on other components. Ace Garage Doors provides professional garage door cable repair and efficient garage door roller replacement to ensure your door moves smoothly and safely along its tracks.

Panel Replacement and Track Alignment

Accidents happen, and sometimes garage door panels can become dented or damaged. We offer seamless garage door panel replacement to restore the aesthetic appeal and structural integrity of your door. If your door is off-track or binding, our technicians can perform precise garage door track alignment to ensure proper movement and prevent further damage.

Weatherstripping and Sealing

Protect your home from the elements and improve energy efficiency with proper garage door weatherstripping. Worn or damaged seals can allow drafts, moisture, and pests to enter your garage. We can replace old weatherstripping to create a tight seal, keeping your garage comfortable and secure year-round.

Comprehensive Garage Door Repair Services

Garage doors are complex systems with many moving parts. When something goes wrong, it can be frustrating and even dangerous. Ace Garage Doors specializes in diagnosing and fixing a wide range of common and uncommon garage door problems. Our expert technicians are skilled in:

Spring Replacements: Torsion & Extension Springs

Broken garage door springs are one of the most common issues homeowners face. These springs are under immense tension and are vital for the balanced operation of your garage door. We offer professional torsion spring replacement and extension spring repair services. Ignoring a broken spring can lead to further damage and safety hazards. If you suspect a spring issue, don't hesitate to call us for a swift and safe repair. We can also provide an estimate for broken spring repair cost.

Opener Repair & Installation: LiftMaster & Chamberlain

Is your garage door opener acting up? Whether you have a LiftMaster opener or a Chamberlain opener, our technicians are trained to repair and install all major brands. We can troubleshoot issues like openers not responding, slow operation, or strange noises. If your opener is beyond repair, we can recommend and install a new, reliable model to enhance your convenience and security.

Cable & Roller Repairs

Worn or broken garage door cables can cause the door to sag or become inoperable. Our team provides expert garage door cable repair to restore proper function. Similarly, damaged or worn-out rollers can lead to noisy operation and difficulty moving the door. We offer efficient garage door roller replacement to ensure smooth, quiet movement.

Panel Replacement & Track Alignment

Accidents happen, and sometimes garage door panels can become dented or damaged. We offer seamless garage door panel replacement to restore the aesthetic appeal and integrity of your door. Misaligned tracks can cause the door to bind or come off its rollers. Our technicians are adept at precise garage door track alignment to prevent further issues.

Weatherstripping & Emergency Services

Protect your home from the elements with proper garage door weatherstripping. Worn-out seals can allow drafts, moisture, and pests to enter your garage. We can replace old weatherstripping to improve your home's energy efficiency and security. For urgent situations, Ace Garage Doors provides reliable emergency garage door service. If your garage door is stuck open or closed, or if it poses a safety risk, call us for same-day garage door repair.

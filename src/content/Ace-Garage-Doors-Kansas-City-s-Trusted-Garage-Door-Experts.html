Expert Garage Door Repair & Maintenance

A malfunctioning garage door can be a major inconvenience and a security risk. We specialize in diagnosing and fixing a wide range of issues to ensure your garage door operates smoothly and safely. Our comprehensive services include:

Torsion Spring Replacement & Extension Spring Repair

Garage door springs are under immense tension and are crucial for the door's operation. If you notice your garage door is difficult to lift or slams shut, you likely need torsion spring replacement or extension spring repair. These are common issues that require professional attention to prevent further damage or injury. We handle all types of spring repairs efficiently.

Garage Door Cable Repair & Roller Replacement

Worn or broken garage door cables can cause the door to become unbalanced or fall. Similarly, damaged rollers can lead to noisy operation and difficulty moving the door. Our technicians are skilled in garage door cable repair and replacing worn-out rollers to restore your door's smooth functionality.

Garage Door Panel Replacement & Track Alignment

Whether your garage door panels are dented, cracked, or damaged by weather, we offer seamless garage door panel replacement to restore its appearance and integrity. We also specialize in precise garage door track alignment to ensure the door moves without obstruction and prevents derailment.

LiftMaster & Chamberlain Opener Services

Is your garage door opener acting up? We are experts in servicing and repairing popular brands like LiftMaster opener and Chamberlain opener systems. From programming remotes to fixing motor issues, we'll get your opener working reliably again.

Garage Door Weatherstripping Installation

Protect your garage and its contents from the elements with proper garage door weatherstripping. We install durable weather seals around your garage door to prevent drafts, moisture, and pests from entering, improving energy efficiency and security.
